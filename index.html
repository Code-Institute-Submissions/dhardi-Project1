Why Should I start Jiu Jitsu?

With crime on the rise, we should all be extra careful – and of course, not take safety for granted – even when we’re in the safest countries. Imagine being caught in a dangerous situation where you have to protect yourself or your loved ones. Would you honestly be able to keep them out of harm’s way? Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, or BJJ, is designed to enable a smaller, weaker person to overcome a bigger, stronger opponent. Since size doesn’t matter, we believe that BJJ is for everyone!

BJJ will build up your cardio endurance, as it involves both your aerobic and anaerobic systems. What’s more, it sculpts your body because you’ll engage different muscle groups when you’re grappling. As you progress in your BJJ journey, you’ll get into your greatest shape, inside and out!

Mindset

Jiu Jitsu promotes a specific mindset that encompasses discipline, perseverance, and problem-solving

Techniques

Technique is at the core of Jiu Jitsu. It emphasizes leverage, timing, and proper body mechanics to overcome physical strength and size disparities.

Physicality

Physicality plays a significant role in Jiu Jitsu. The art emphasizes the development of strength, endurance, flexibility, and body awareness.
